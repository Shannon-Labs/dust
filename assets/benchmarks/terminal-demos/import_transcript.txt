
--- 1. Initialize project ---

$ dust init .
Initialized Dust project at .

--- 2. Generate sample CSV ---

$ cat employees.csv
id,name,department,salary
1,Alice,Engineering,120000
2,Bob,Marketing,95000
3,Carol,Engineering,115000
4,Dave,Sales,88000
5,Eve,Engineering,130000
6,Frank,Marketing,92000
7,Grace,Sales,97000

--- 3. Import CSV into Dust ---

$ dust import employees.csv
Imported 7 rows into `employees` (4 columns)
Columns: id, name, department, salary

--- 4. Explore the imported data ---

$ dust query "SELECT * FROM employees"
id	name	department	salary
1	Alice	Engineering	120000
2	Bob	Marketing	95000
3	Carol	Engineering	115000
4	Dave	Sales	88000
5	Eve	Engineering	130000
6	Frank	Marketing	92000
7	Grace	Sales	97000
$ dust query "SELECT department, count(*) AS headcount FROM employees GROUP BY department"
department	headcount
Engineering	3
Marketing	2
Sales	2

--- 5. Check project status ---

$ dust status
Branch: main
Database: ./.dust/workspace/data.db
Schema: sch_d87554f2b948

Tables:
  employees: 7 rows

Size: 32.0 KB

Done. CSV to queryable table in one command.
