Waitlist

The Team beta is qualification-first.

Dust is not pretending the beta is self-serve. The waitlist path is designed to capture enough context to qualify real use cases before inviting teams into a manual onboarding flow.

Team beta intake

Use the public intake form when the goal is a real beta evaluation of Dust with a specific team workflow in mind.

  • team or company name
  • contact details
  • current database stack
  • primary use case and urgency
  • team size and rollout shape

Enterprise contact

Use the enterprise path when the conversation is about a design-partner relationship, rollout shape, or support expectations.

  • company and primary contact
  • current environment and constraints
  • evaluation timeline
  • architecture or support expectations
