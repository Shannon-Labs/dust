Terms

The public launch surface should be explicit about what exists today and what remains beta or roadmap.

  • The CLI is offered as open-source software under the repository license terms.
  • Invite-only commercial surfaces remain manually operated until the billing path is fully automated.
  • Nothing on the site should imply SLA, hosting guarantees, or GA availability that the project does not actually provide.
